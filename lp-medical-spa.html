Meet Our BTL
Specialist

Heather Jane “HJ” McHugh has helped more than 700 patients with Med Spa services. She is double board-certified by the American Academy of Procedural Medicine in Aesthetics, and she is committed to providing clients safe, accessible, and high quality care from consultation to end of session. An eastern Connecticut-native, HJ received her B.S. & M.S. in Nursing from University of Connecticut (Storrs, CT). Upon completion of her Masters program as an Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner, HJ served 2 years as a Hospitalist at a local community hospital and received the Mid-level Provider of the Year award in 2016.
